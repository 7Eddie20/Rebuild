## 💡 Key Learnings  

- `cin >>` reads tokens; `getline()` reads full lines including spaces  
- `cin.ignore()` is essential when mixing input methods  
- The art of programming lies in clarity, not just syntax  
- Thinking in flowcharts or pseudocode first helps avoid logic bugs later
